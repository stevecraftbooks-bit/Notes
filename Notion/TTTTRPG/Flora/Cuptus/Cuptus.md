Cuptus are a type of cactus found to grow in a [[Black Desert]]. They are discerned by the all white, round bulbs that modularly grow off the main shoot. These bulbous structures can detect when it rains and open a dome-like hemisphere to let the form catch rain like a cup. During the day, if there is no rain the cups stay closed to keep from loosing as much water as possible. The outside of the plant is covered in spines to protect it from general harm or spillage while it is closed. This environment is also welcome to other small fauna who might need a bath or place to rest for a while. Issues may arise if a creature is taking shelter and the area encounters a dry spell. This can cause creatures to starve within the cavity, potentially giving the plant nutrients or feeding other creatures of the environment. Although the Cuptus have many unwanted residents, the water it stores is generally filtered through their thick vascular network. This water is full of nutrients from the plant and is usually slightly sweet and extremely refreshing.
