# Blades, Toolheads

Affects damage type and amount. Adds extra die and new damage types, or adds offensive abilities/on hit effects based on material origins.

# Guards, Grips, Chains

Affects ability to doge attacks while holding. Affects strength or dexterity checks using the tool or weapon. Adds defensive abilities/on doge effects based on material origins.

# Handles

Affects ability to hit a target. Gives advantage during certain conditions based on material origin. Gives disadvantages respectfully as well. Adds environmental abilities/summoning effects based on material origin.
