- Nomadic, small, green, thieves

- They live and travel in packs and families and occasionally settle in caves to form small civilizations.

- Usually set up camps on long journeys to nearby other civilizations.

- They steal resources of other civilizations to fuel and feed their civilizations.

- Their social status has thin lines, but is usually ties with size and amount of food they eat.

They wear wooden horns with different colored scavenged flowers to determine social status:

Purple-King/Blue-Noble/Red-Common Man
