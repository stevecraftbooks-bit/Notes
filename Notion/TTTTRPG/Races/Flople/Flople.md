- Flople's born in the water are half bipedal and half fish.

- Their size can vary depending on the type of fish.

- Although they can live on both land and in water, they prefer the safer and more food abundant oceans to strive.

- They know very little of land culture and don't know what most things are.

- They obsess over shiny objeots.

- Horns are made from scavenged metals off the sea floor, the horns shininess indicates status:

Polished-God/shiny-King/brushed-Noble/rusted-Common Man
