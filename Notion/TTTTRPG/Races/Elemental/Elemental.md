- Elementals have or strive to have mastery over the element of their construct.

- Elements origins are dependent on their type:

- Water; Ocean (Ocean rock)

- Fire; Hotspots (Coals)

- Earth; Underground (Bedrocks)

- Wind; Atop Mountains (Fine Gravels)

- They are born when a previous elemental has died, maintain population. This sense of reincarnation passes down a little knowledge every time making them desire information.

- They form small civilizations in these regions of birth.

- Their horns are made of rock from the origins and grow bigger as they gain more knowledge.

- Size of the horns indicate both social status and power

---

- Elementals are not always humanoid. In a majority of occurrences they are other forms to focus on themselves.

- It’s harder to communicate with them in these forms as they don’t speak or understand any common language of humanoids.

- Any elemental in these forms tend to be neutral or hostile towards humanoids, they tend to not be friendly to focus on themselves.
